About Us

To be in the Top Quartile of Category 1 MFIs in Cameroon.

To provide its members with an efficient and sustainable platform of financial products and services at competitive market conditions in an enabling and conducive environment that allows them to develop their full potential

The purpose of this union is:

  • To promote thrift among its members, by offering them an opportunity to accumulate their savings.
  • To create for them a source of credit for provident productive purposes at fair and reasonable rates of interest.
  • To provide the opportunity for members to use and control their money for their mutual benefits.
  • Generally, to promote the social and economic interest of its members, community and the country as a whole.

PECCU is dedicated to helping small businesses

  • Line of credit for business accounts

  • Clearing and discounting of cheques and bills issued by reputable companies

  • Buying and Servicing of External debts from local financial institutions or Banks
