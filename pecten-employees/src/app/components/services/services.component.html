Ordinary Shares Account

This type of shares qualifies you, a joint owner of PECCU along with other members, and gives you the right to vote and to be voted as a Board member or committee member of PECCU at the Annual General Meeting. This shares account yield interest, but jointly calculated with interest on savings payable at the end of each year.

Variable shares

This type of shares is same like Ordinary shares, but available to members who would like to increase their shareholding in PECCU.

Redeemable Preference Shares

This type of shares yields interest, One RPS is 1,500,000FCFA. Maturity period for RPS investment is 10 years minimum with fixed interest rate of 3% per annum.

Savings Account

For a member to be considered active and regular in PECCU he or she must save at least 25,000FCFA per month. Prompt cash Withdrawals from savings accounts attract a charge of 5%, except a member gives a notice to withdraw from savings. (501,000 to 5 million one month notice, from 5 million to 10 million two months’ notice, from 10 million above three months’ notice).

Deposit / Current Account

This account does not bear interest; it helps members build up financial resources for short and medium term needs. Cash withdrawals from deposits accounts do not attract charges, except annual charges of 5000 FRS.

Term Deposits

Members who do not like to presently use their savings and money kept in different accounts or held in other financial institutions; are advised to transform it into term deposit against a certificate of deposit for an attractive annual interest rate of 5%.

Line of credit for business accounts

To obtain this loan, a member is required to have 10% of the loan in savings account. Member is expected to increase savings every month. Interest rate 11.3% per annum, tax free on loan.

Building or House Purchase

To obtain this loan, a member is required to have 20% of the loan in the savings account as down payment, amongst others at an Interest rate of 6.5% per annum, tax free for any amount above 20million. For any amount below 20 Million the interest rate is 8.1% per annum tax free.

Buying and Servicing of External debts from local financial institutions or Banks

Golden Loan

This is 'huge loan' for projects.

Christmas Savings Account

To ease members from end of year financial stress, PECCU operates a Christmas Savings Account whereby members save regularly and withdraw from it first week of December. These savings earn interest at 2%.

OTHER SERVICES

Other services we offer are done through partnership. A good example is our partnership with UBA Bank to offer PECCU BRANDED VISA CARD SERVICES. This requires an upfront Payment of 13,000 FCFA to get a card (Zero VAT). There is an annual Charge of 8,000 FCFA (Zero VAT) and a withdrawing charge of 500 FCFA from any local ATM per transaction and Zero charge for withdrawal within UBA ATM. You can also make an international withdrawal, which will cost you 2,500 FCFA per transaction.

PECCU is an affiliate of the Cameroon Co-operative Credit Union League (CamCCUL), which is the oldest and largest federation of Credit Unions in Cameroon. CamCCUL is an umbrella organization belonging to its members and provides services such as education and training, management control through regular and periodic auditing, representation, Investment services and advice, etc to the members. CamCCUL is registered and recognized in the Ministries of Agriculture, the Economy and Finance (Decision No. 00395/MINEFI of August 20, 2001) and, by COBAC under decision No. D-2001/05, code No. 19419 of January 11, 2001. PECCU being affiliated to and controlled by CamCCUL, is therefore automatically accredited by the same bodies mentioned above to perform savings and credit activities in accordance with the Micro Finance Laws and Regulations in Cameroon.
