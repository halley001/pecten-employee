How to become a member

This is an official request for everyone to join us and enjoy all the benefi ts we off er. We take pride in saying we are one of the best credit unions in Cameroon. With 30 years of expertise and services, we are sure every new member will feel secured into our organisation. Our conditions are listed below. Our offices are opened for anyone who requires more clarifications or information about other opportunities with PECCU.

We have listed below in detail what you would need to become a member of PECCU. However, if you would like to find out more, visit our website www.peccu.org or visit our offices at Bonanjo, Douala.

  • A photocopy of identity card/ passport
  • Two passport sized photographs,
  • A copy of your water/ electricity bills,
  • Photocopy of last three months’ pay slips, Fill a PECCU membership application form, and pay the following:
  • 50,000 FCFA Entrance fees.
  • 100,000 FCFA Ordinary shares, refundable
  • 40,000 FCFA Solidarity fund.
  • 200,000FCFA building fee (payable in 20 instalments of 10,000×20)
  • 25,000 FCFA minimum savings every month
