Pecten Employees Coorperative Credit Union Celebrates 30 Years of Great Achievement

We are pleased to announce that PECCU, the Pecten Employee Cooperative Credit Union is celebrating its 30TH Anniversary. This great event will be marked by a series of activities to .celebrate the success and the achievements of this financial institution from its creation till date. PECCU is celebrating 30 years of excellence and we believe for this celebration to be successful, our media friends have to join us to share their success story.

PECCU is a financial Co-operative Credit Union owned and managed by its members. Founded by the Employees of PECTEN CAMEROON COMPANY, its first registration was in 1986 and re-registered in 2001. The main purpose of PECCU is to give the possibility to members in accumulating their savings, create a source of credit, promote social and economic growth and interest of its member and the community. The whole event will consist in a three phase celebration on two days:

A Charity Walk on Saturday February 20, 2016, during which funds will be raised to support an orphanage. This will be followed by a soccer match at the Addax Basement. There will be two matches, one by men and the other by women, all members, executive bureau and well-wishers of PECCU. These will be another way to connect with the community. A Press Conference will be held on the 24th February at Ibis Hotel, Douala so as to share the success story of this financial institution across the whole world. The 26th February, 2016 calls for celebration as a Gala Party in happiness and pride to celebrate 30 years of excellence and success.

The guests’ dynamics of the PECCU 30th Anniversary are members, EXCO, government officials and all PECCU well-wishers. Join them in celebrating in grand style and festivity great moments of this final institution’s life and history.
