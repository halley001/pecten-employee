Who We Are

PECCU is a financial Co-operative Credit Union owned and managed by its members. It was founded by the Employees of PECTEN CAMEROON COMPANY. It's first registration was in 1986 and re-gistered in 2001 under Decision N°00395/MINIFI, and the Banking Commission for Central African States (COBAC) under Decision N°D-2001/05 - Code N°19419.

PECCU is affliliated to the Cameroon Co-operative Credit Union Leaque (CamCCUL).
