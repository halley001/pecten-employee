Ongoing School Fees Loans!

We all as parents want to equip our children with the best education that will set them upon the path to a successful life. In our commitment to helping you provide all that is best for your little loved ones, we have – once more – launched our school fees loans program for the upcoming 2016/2017 academic year!

How does it work?

When you meet the requirements to benefit from our school fees loans, we pay the total fees requesteddirectly to youvia electronic transfer. This is a convenient way to manage school fees, especially for school that request urge amounts with very few or no instalments. You then have the opportunity to spread the cost of school fees with affordable monthly repayments to us.

How much will it cost you?

We offer highly competitive and affordable rates for our school fees loans. Get in touch with us and you will find out how surprisingly low they are.

What do you need to apply?

Applying is very easy. All you need is meet the following requirements:

  1. Be a registered Member of PECCU
  2. Possess the flexible Capacity of repayment
  3. Document evident of school fees need
  4. Apply for a loan amount within you credit capacity

How long does it take?

Once you meet all requirements, it usually takes less than 48 hoursto obtain a school fees loan from us.
